Prompt: Free Misfortune
Theme: Pirates/Seafaring

This sucks. You would think that pirates wouldn't have such strong feelings against thievery, since that's part of their job description. But no, they apparently take it quite personally when someone lifts a coin or ten from their treasure chest when they aren't looking. Hypocrites, I say.

So anyway, I'm currently running for my life through the back alleys of our dear port city. Hot on my tail are three very angry pirates. I hope these coins are worth it. I'm not an appraiser, but they sure are shiny. Especially this larger one that has writing on it.

Meaning: What is the inscription? [Curses: Change / Intellect] Making me dumber seems too obvious and not fun. I'll say it alters my perception somehow instead.
Meaning: How? [Powers: Death / Cold] I see dead people.

I can't make out the inscription, and definitely not while trying to escape certain death, but just touching the coin sends a cold chill through my body. I'm just going to assume that feeling was only my excitement for the impending pay day when I cash this sucker in.

Straight ahead the alley splits into a tee, and I dash into the narrower alley on the right, hoping that the muscled baffoons will struggle to squeeze through the same tight spaces that my small body can.

Action: Attempting to outrun them. (hide in a crowd) [3, 1] + 1 = 5. Miss. They catch up to me.

My hopes are dashed and the pirates follow me just fine, kicking boxes and other debris out of the way as they sprint after me. Even worse, this narrow alley is a damned dead end and I am soon to be boxed in.

"We got you now, girlie!" one of the grubby pirates calls after me.

Fate Chart: Do I see an exit? (50/50) [90] No. I'll have to face them.

As I come to a stop at the end of the alley, I grip the coins tightly in my hand and glance around in a panic. There are no exits or any obvious means over the wall that I am facing. There are a couple doors leading into the surrounding buildings back the way I came, but I had run past them like an idiot and now the pirates are between me and them.

The cold chill in my hand has grown intense, feeling as though I'm holding an icicle. I hold up the coin with the inscription and see that a cold mist is falling from it. Something in the shadows just beyond the quickly approaching pirates catches my attention and I peer into them.

Action: Draw the spirits. (cursed coin, spirit awareness, draw spirits / tenacious) [1, 6] + 2 = 9. Mixed hit. The spirits come, but not in time.

The shadows take shape, coalescing into grotesque humanoid forms.
