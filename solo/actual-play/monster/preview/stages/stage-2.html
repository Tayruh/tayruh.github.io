Chapter 5

*1 Having appeased Gray like he were a damn bridge troll, he finally granted us passage, allowing us to continue on our way. Finola chose to stay behind with the bogeyman, saying that he would be more fun than watching us do work. I wasn't sure whether that meant he'd be her pranking victim or accomplice, but Seoyun and I left her to it.

The two of us arrived at the kitchen at long last, completing our quest for my dear sister, who appeared to be bustling away at assembling a large snack platter.

Seoyun and I drop off the supplies.

Erin: "Great. You're back." She grinned at me. "Any trouble getting the stuff?"
Seoyun: "Only that boogerman asshat getting in our faces, like usual."
Erin: "Ugh. I hate that guy." Her shoulders drooped and she sighed. "I actually really love this job, except for him."
Seoyun: "He harasses you because you and Seth are the only humans around, and Seth is nearly impossible to scare."
Me: "What do you think about maybe having Melody come visit?" It wasn't my smoothest segue, but I wanted to know.
*2 Erin: She blinked. "That's a horrible idea."
Seoyun: She laughed. "Oh, Gray isn't going to like that answer."
Me: I glared at Seoyun, then turned back to Erin. "Are you sure? Wouldn't it be nice to have a human friend around, though?"

*3 Jess overhears this, having entered the kitchen to grab something for Cami.

Jess: "Are you serious?" I turned to look at her, and saw that she was glaring straight at me. "It's not enough for you to replace me as my mistress's thrall, you need to replace me as Erin's friend too?"

*4 Jess launches herself at me. *5 I grab her as she leaps on me, and I use her momentum to flip her behind me. She crashes into the cupboards, shattering plates. *6 She doesn't waste a second before she rebounds off the ruined cupboard and throws a punch at me.

Jess: "Die, you bastard!"
Erin: "Jess, stop!"

*7 Seoyun grapples Jess from behind before she can land the hit, and I take advantage of their struggle to snag a frying pan off the counter and swing it at Jess's head.

Erin: "Seth!"

*8 Jess is unable to free herself in time and I get a clean hit. The pan smashes into her skull hard enough to put a dent in the pan's surface. Jess would have dropped to her knees, were she not still in Seoyun's hold.

*9 Erin: "Please, just stop! You're being stupid. I can have more than one friend. Being friends with Melody doesn't take away from our friendship."
*10 Jess: "But I've seen the way my mistress looks at him." She was practically growling. "Camellia is mine. I won't let anyone have her!"
Me: "Jess, I don't even plan on dating Cami. I definitely don't have any desire to be her servant for an eternity. That position is all yours."
Seoyun: "Besides, Seth only has eyes for me. Don't you, sweetie?"

I don't waste my breathe responding.

*11 Jess: The fight had gone out of her, but she muttered to me, "I don't believe you."
Me: I groaned. "This is why I wanted Erin to have human friends. Your supernatural bond to Cami makes you unstable and dangerous."
Jess: "I am human!" Then in a quieter voice, she said, "I'm not unstable."

*12 I dismissed Jess's comment. I knew that she was taking Cami's blood to prevent aging, so that she could stay by her side throughout the centuries. The enhanced abilities that it gave her were just a side effect, as was her obsessed devotion to the vampire princess, I suspected. It still felt like splitting hairs to me though.

Me: "Just forget it." I sighed. "Erin's right. Bringing Melody here is a bad idea."

And she was right. Not just because Melody would be in danger, but because I was choosing to put her in danger so that Erin wouldn't be. That kind of action was not me. I was angry at myself for having buckled under the weight of Gray's threat. There had to be another way to protect my sister.

Seoyun was frowning at me. I think she was as concerned as I was at how Gray would take my change of heart. I guess we would find out soon enough. *13
