Magik

  • Illyana Rasputin
  • Power: A Class. Teleporting and magic.
  • Hero: Vengeful wielder of the Soulsword.
  • Trouble: Sorceress Supreme of Limbo.
  • Superb (+5): Fight
  • Great (+4): Athleticism, Willpower
  • Good (+3): Physique, Presence, Provoke
  • Attribute Bonuses: Athleticism +1, Physique +1/+2 (WR2/AR1 or WR4/AR2 with armor), Presence +1, Willpower +1/+3 (WR2/AR1 or WR8/AR4 with magic)
  • Fate Points (4): 4
  • Physical Stress: 1 2 3 4
  • Mental Stress: 1 2 3 4
  • Consequences
    • Mild (2):
    • Moderate (4):
    • Severe (6):
  • Stunts
    • Magic: Illyana learned a combination of light and dark magic while in Limbo and furthered her studies with Doctor Strange. She is considered an A Class magic user. When using magic, her attribute and bonuses reflect a +4 increase to Willpower. (8)
    • Stepping Disc: Illyana can create a circular disc anywhere and at any angle (it does not require a surface). Stepping through the disc teleports her anywhere in space and time, and she can do this multiple times in a row, but she must always travel to Limbo before her final destination.
    • Limbo Blast: Illyana can fire large blasts of energy from Limbo using the stepping disc portals, which function as a floating energy cannon with WR 2 for the cost of a Fate Point. For 2 Fate Points, she can hit multiple targets at once without dividing her Successes.
    • Mystical Eldritch Armor: Illyana's armor grants her super strength. She can only use the armor while she has possession of her Soulsword. For 1 Fate Point she can conjure the armor and gain +2 Physique while it's active. The armor lasts until the Scene ends, it's dispelled, or Illyana falls unconscious.
    • Soulsword: Illyana's sword is formed from a piece of her soul. It is not physically real, and therefore can only harm magical creatures. It does WR 2, adds +1 to Fight, and benefits from Illyana's magic though; it does not benefit from Physique. Because the sword is a piece of her soul, anyone in possession of the Soulsword has basic access to Illyana's abilities--namely magic and creating stepping discs.
  • Permissions/Denials
    • Portals: Illyana's stepping disc portal can remain open as long as she wants and has been seen to grow large enough to swallow the X-Mansion. The portal opens quickly enough that she can use it as a means of dodging and countering in combat. While Illyana has been known to travel as far as the other side of the globe, traveling so far puts a strain on her ability and she runs the risk of arriving in the wrong place or even the wrong time.
    • Demon Queen: Illyana is the ruler of Limbo and can summon demons via her stepping disc to fight by her side.
    • Darkchylde: When Illyana is overcome with emotions such as anger, she "hulks out" and becomes Darkchylde, which is the form of the demon queen of Limbo. She has difficulty maintaining control over her mind and emotions in this form and basically becomes another personality. While in this form, she denies any attempts of telepathic probing used against her. Illyana will revert to normal when rendered unconscious.

  • Stunts
    • Superhuman Physique +4: Rogue is an A Class bruiser. (8)

    • Energy Drain: Touching skin causes a fixed amount of 4 Physical Stress for every turn that she is touching. This Stunt triggers Power Absorbion for free and also shuts down that mutant's powers temporarily, whether intended or not.

    • Touch: Rogue mostly has control over her energy drain power activating by touch these days, but it can accidentally be triggered when she's scared.

It's uncertain whether these changes came from being taught by teachers more experienced than Xavier was back then, or if it has been brought about by the increased level of combat that present day X-Men face. Either way, her powers are quickly manifesting and it's become a concern for X-Men both from the past and present day.
