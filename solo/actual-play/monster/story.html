Characters: Add Mansion, Erin, Walter, Seoyun, Sera, Finola, Vanessa, Gray, Cami, and Jess.
Chaos Factor: Set to 5.

First Scene: [PC Positive: Celebrate Work] We're celebrating a year of everyone being together.

It's been a full year since Erin and I took the position of housemaster for this mansion converted into a boarding house. The role was bestowed upon us by our dear father, who immediately ran off to who-knows-where and left my sister and I to run this place by ourselves with no prior experience. However it wasn't the lack of experience in running a boarding house that was our biggest hurdle, but rather the inexperience with handling monsters.

To our surprise, there is an entire world hidden from most of humanity. Mythological monsters are real. They are called "myths" and try to hide from the public when they cannot blend. Once upon a time, they have would have ruled man, but man has advanced and now has the power to eradicate their kind. And so, they hide.

This boarding house provides shelter for these myths. Apparently, it is a traditional that's been passed on for generations on our father's side of the family. A fact that Erin and I were not made aware of until immediately before our father sacked us with these duties and then disappeared.
