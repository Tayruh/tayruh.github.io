I waited nervously for someone to answer the door. I just had to see him again. My love, my sweet. It had been too long since I'd last seen his face. Nearly a whole twenty-four hours. It felt like I was dying.

Which they could, cuz that was his super power. His face, though, was that of a supermodel. Like a manly one, you know? With a strong chin and a stern brow, permanently frozen in a furrow of disapproval. So judgemental and sexy, my sweetums. He was a true hero.

Oh, you didn't think that I'd be dumb enough to knock on the door of the largest, most renowned government-funded superhero team in the world, did you? Ha! No way.
