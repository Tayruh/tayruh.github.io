PATHS
Armored
  • When you Face Danger, React Under Fire, or Clash against physical attacks or impacts, you may put trust in your armor's strength. If you do, preset your action die to 4. On a strong hit with match, take +2 momentum as you build confidence, make an impression on your foes, or improve your position.

Empath
  • When you read the intent, emotions, or memories of a nearby being, roll +heart. On a strong hit, you glimpse a helpful aspect of their inner self. Envision what you learn, take +2 momentum, and add +1 when you make moves to interact with them in this scene. On a weak hit, the visions are murky; take +1 momentum. On a miss, you reveal a troubling motive or secret; Pay the Price.

COMPANIONS
Bugs (custom): 2/2 health
  • You can control bugs with your mind and send a unique command to each individual bug. You can amass them into decoys of yourself, speak through them by altering their buzzing, or have them manipulate small objects. When you make a move by instructing your bugs to perform a task, add +its health.
  • Through your bugs you are also able to perceive sight, sound, and touch, as well as always knowing the exact location of every bug in relation to yourself. When you make a move that is aided by your swarm's senses or its relayed position, add +its health.
  • Your swarm can attack with bites and stings, enter eyes and airways, entangle with lines of webbing, and sabotage wiring. However, bugs will always be sacrificed during the attack. When you Strike or Clash by instructing your swarm to harm or obstruct, add +its health and then suffer -1 health to your bugs; take +1 momentum on hit. With a moment’s rest, you can gather more bugs to your swarm, returning it automatically to max health.
